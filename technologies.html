What methods of learning have you found most useful in your career?

Early on in my career, having a foundational knowledge of IT really helped me get started. I obtained that through a combination of on-the-job training as well as formal learning (e.g., TAFE diplomas, university degree).

As my career progressed, and as IT evolved, there's now a greater value placed on certifications (depending on what part of IT you work in). You can achieve Certifications quickly, and you can obtain multiple certifications to build up your knowledge in certain areas.
